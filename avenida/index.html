Projection Mapping: Avenida Theatre

Contemporary tribute to the city's rich heritage

Created to celebrate Tetouan’s designation as the 2026 Cultural Capital, this projection mapping installation transforms the historic façade of the Avenida Theatre. By merging dynamic visuals with synchronized soundscapes, the artwork serves as a contemporary tribute to the city's rich heritage. It bridges enduring historical legacy with digital innovation, breathing vibrant new life into an iconic architectural landmark.
